Into the mist

So, you’re probably wondering where I'm heading to. well, I am headed to Minnoush Michigan! Yoopers shay hello!  This wonderful 1600-person city is right on Lake Michigan, in the heart of the rust belt.  On their website it says that the city “has a lively downtown, friendly people, and a large nature preserve.” apparently they have at least some tourist passing though because there are a handful of hotels in the area. mostly single night motels but I was able to find a bed and breakfast within my budget, it looks lovely from the pictures, apparently it was once a townhouse that the owner has changed to be a BnB. It is called Gilman House, and here is its website.

This town called to me when I read about the recent shutting down of its largest factory during the 2008 recession.  It was hit hard outside of that, with the town being hit hard by the past winters and a large exodus with the closing of the factory.  Though recently it has been hit with the urban explorer craze and is leaning into that along with improving its public areas, namely its large game reserve.  Though I'm not going to be doing any hunting there are nature trails throughout the park along with tours.

Thank you to Juussttmeeee for bringing this wonderful town to my attention. I cannot wait to explore it! also, feel free to dm me so we can get some drinks while I'm here!

Anyway, this is day 0, there will be another post or two as long as the bus’s wifi holds out.  see you soon!



LOL my bus driver is from Minnoush!

Haha, he’s a really cool guy we during the stopover in Oldburry we had a nice chat. He was born and raised in Minnosh. He was able to tell me about the town’s history. Apparently, it was some sort of fishing town before the factory, and some local businesses are trying to bring fishing back as a new income source. Which I think is super rad! The lake trout and whitefish are some of the best natural recourses from this area, and I cannot wait to see what these guys are bringing in!
